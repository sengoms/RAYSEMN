Our Mission

RAYSE is dedicated to breaking down barriers in STEM education by creating inclusive, supportive environments where every young person can thrive. We believe that diversity drives innovation and that every student deserves access to high-quality STEM learning opportunities.

Through our FIRST LEGO League (FLL), FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), Drone Racing, and RC Planes programs, we're building the next generation of problem-solvers, innovators, and leaders who will shape our future.

FIRST LEGO League (FLL)

Perfect for students ages 9-14, our FLL teams combine the excitement of sport with science and technology. Teams research real-world problems and build LEGO robots to solve challenges.

What Students Learn:

  • Engineering design process
  • Programming and robotics
  • Research and presentation skills
  • Teamwork and communication
  • Problem-solving strategies

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)

For students ages 12-18, FTC takes robotics to the next level with more complex challenges, advanced programming, and real-world engineering applications.

What Students Learn:

  • Advanced programming (Java/Blocks)
  • CAD design and 3D printing
  • Project management
  • Community outreach
  • Business and marketing skills

Drone Racing Club

Experience the thrill of high-speed drone racing while learning cutting-edge technology. Students ages 10-18 build, program, and pilot custom racing drones in competitive events.

What Students Learn:

  • Drone assembly and maintenance
  • Flight control programming
  • Aerodynamics and physics
  • Real-time piloting skills
  • Competition strategy

RC Planes Club

Explore the fundamentals of aviation through radio-controlled aircraft. Students ages 8-18 learn aerodynamics, electronics, and flight principles while building and flying RC planes.

What Students Learn:

  • Aerodynamics and flight physics
  • Radio control systems
  • Aircraft construction techniques
  • Flight planning and safety
  • Weather impact on flight
